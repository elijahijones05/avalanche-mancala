Avalanche

Sow the stones. Trigger an avalanche. Sweep the board.

How Avalanche Mancala works
  • Pick a pit on your side. Its stones drop one-by-one into each following pit, moving counter-clockwise.
  • If your last stone lands in a pit that already has stones, scoop up the whole pit and keep sowing — that's the avalanche.
  • Land your last stone in your own store to go again.
  • Land your last stone in an empty pit — yours or your opponent's — and your turn simply ends there.
  • When one side runs out of pits to play, the game ends and the other player sweeps their remaining stones home.
